Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Internship CRM.

Is Internship CRM really free?

Yes, Internship CRM is completely free for students. We believe in helping students land great internships without adding financial stress. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

Do you store my personal data?

We take privacy seriously. We only store the information necessary to track your applications (company name, position, application date, status). Your resume and cover letters are processed locally on your device using Google's Gemini API and are never stored on our servers.

Which job sites are supported?

We support all major job boards including LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Handshake, and company career pages. Our system automatically detects application forms and job descriptions across the web. If you find a site that doesn't work, let us know and we'll add support!

How good are the AI-generated cover letters?

Our AI cover letters are powered by Google's Gemini API and are highly personalized based on the job description and your resume. They're designed to highlight relevant skills and experiences while matching the company's tone. You can edit them before sending, but many students use them as-is with great results.

Can I use this for full-time jobs too?

Absolutely! While we've optimized for internship applications, Internship CRM works just as well for entry-level and full-time positions. Many recent graduates continue using our tool as they transition into the job market.
